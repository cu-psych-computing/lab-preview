The Plasticity of Cognitive Maps Lab

The Plasticity of Cognitive Maps Lab is currently seeking research assistants

Research Projects

The Plasticity of Cognitive Maps Lab focuses on understanding strategies used by humans when learning spatial awareness and spatial relations. Ongoing projects consist of implementing models of learning in children diagnosed with ASD. Opportunities in the lab include running participants (e.g., behavioral tasks), design of methodologies and data analysis, and collaborative works with other institutions and PIs.
