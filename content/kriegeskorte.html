The Visual Inference Lab

The Visual Inference Lab is currently seeking research assistants

Lab description

The Visual Inference Lab studies the brain information processing that enables visual perception, object recognition, and scene understanding. A core goal of the lab is to build neural network models to predict human and animal performance in visual tasks and the concomitant brain activity. Our research involves neural network modeling, measurement of behavior and brain activity in human and nonhuman primates, and development of statistical analysis and visualization techniques. Additional information about our research is available at our lab website
