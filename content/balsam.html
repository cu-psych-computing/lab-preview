The Adaptive Behavior Lab

The Adaptive Behavior Lab is not currently seeking research assistants

The Adaptive Behavioral Laboratory, under the direction of Peter Balsam, studies how animals learn the relationships between important events. Using classical and operant conditioning procedures in rodents, we are interested in the behavioral and neural mechanisms of learning and motivation. You can learn more about what we do on our lab website: http://www.columbia.edu/cu/psychology/balsam/index.html We may be looking for additinoal research assistants.
